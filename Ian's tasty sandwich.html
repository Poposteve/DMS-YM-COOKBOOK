The Tasty Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Everything Bagel
  • 3 slices of ham
  • 3 slices of salami
  • 1 egg
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Take an everything bagel and put mayo on the inside of both halves
  2. Put 3 slices of ham and salami on one half of the bagel
  3. place a slice of swiss cheese on the other half
  4. Put these in a toaster oven and cook for about 3 minutes
  5. Simultaneously fry a single egg
  6. Once the egg and the bagel are done cooking put the egg in the bagel and lightly add salt and pepper

    7. This sandwich is the culmination of years of progress and many lunchs trying different ingredients. I hope you enjoy it!
